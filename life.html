Sergey was born in the Soviet Union in 1973, with his parents emigrating to the United States when he was 6. Fearing persecution by the Soviet government, his parents felt they would have a better life in the United States. Sergey said in an interview with Mark Malseed for The Google Story that his father, Mikhail Brin, was "forced to abandon his dream of becoming an astronomer even before he reached college"(1). His father went on to become a professor of mathematics at the University of Maryland, and instilled in Sergey a love of mathematics and computing from a young age. After graduating from Eleanor Roosevelt High School in 1990, Sergey followed in both his fathers and his grandfathers footsteps by going on to study Mathematics with Computer Science at the University of Maryland.

After graduating from the University of Maryland, Sergey went on to being studying for his Phd and Stanford University. It was here where he met Larry Page, someone who in theory was the intellectual opposite of him, where the two fast became friends.
